node_copy: The RRAAE is Ecuador’s national search engine, aimed at facilitating the management, decentralization, organization, preservation, and interoperability of open-access digital content produced by the country’s academic and scientific community. It promotes the visibility of knowledge production distributed across various institutional repositories nationwide, contributing to the development of education in the country. All members of CEDIA participate, as well as other institutions that wish to make their academic and scientific output available to the rest of the country.
