node_copy: The SNRD promotes open access in Argentina and the sharing of the scientific and technological output produced in the country. It is an interoperable network of digital repositories in science and technology in Argentina, established through the implementation of common policies, standards, and protocols for all members of the system. Currently, the system contains more than 400,000 digital objects from 44 Argentine repositories. This initiative is led by the Ministry of Science, Technology, and Innovation in collaboration with the Interinstitutional Council of Science and Technology (CICyT) through its representatives on the Advisory Council of the Electronic Library of Science and Technology.
