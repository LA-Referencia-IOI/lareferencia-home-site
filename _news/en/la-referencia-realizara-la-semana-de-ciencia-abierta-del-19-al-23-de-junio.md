LA Reference, together with its National Nodes, will carry out the **LA Reference Open Science Week 2023. The activity will have more than 20 presentations that will expose the development of Open Science in the Latin American region and Spain.**

The conferences will take place **from next Monday, June 19 to Friday, June 23 through [LA Reference YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@RepositoriosLA/streams)**. The activity has no cost and anyone can participate by simply logging in at the time of interest.

[See the full program here](https://recursos.lareferencia.info/semana-de-ciencia-abierta-la-referencia-2023/)
