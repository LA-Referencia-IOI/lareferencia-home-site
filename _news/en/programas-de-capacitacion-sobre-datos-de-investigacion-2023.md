LA Referencia, together with the Research Data Alliance Nodo Costa Rica and the National Council of Rectors (CONARE) of Costa Rica, **will offer two training programs on the topic of Research Data in the month of July.**

**Programs will be offered by Heather Andrews, Data Stewardship at the Technical University of Delft, Netherlands. These are asynchronous trainings through explanatory videos plus a live question and answer session with the trainer.**

#### How to participate?

The person can take only one or both training programs, for this they must watch the videos listed below and participate in the live question and answer session with the trainer on the day detailed. The session will be broadcast openly through [LA Referencia YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@RepositoriosLA/streams)

#### Question and Answer Session Dates:

* Training Program on Research Data Management in Scientific Practice: Tuesday, July 11 (9 a.m. Costa Rica and Mexico, 10 a.m. Panama, Peru, Colombia and Ecuador, 11 a.m. Chile, 12 p.m. Argentina, Brazil and Uruguay and 5 p.m. Spain).
* Research Data Planning Training Program: Tuesday, July 18 (9 a.m. Costa Rica and Mexico, 10 a.m. Panama, Peru, Colombia and Ecuador, 11 a.m. Chile, 12 p.m. Argentina, Brazil and Uruguay and 5 p.m. Spain).

#### About the certificate of participation:

A certificate of participation will be offered to those who complete the form that will be shared during the Question and Answer Session.
