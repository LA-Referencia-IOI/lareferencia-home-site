LA Reference, COAR and Lyrasis (DSpace) **collaborate on policies to support institutional repositories in the Latin American region** to face the necessary technological update in the face of the new challenges of Open Science.

In that sense, **in order to know in more detail the state of the situation and the needs of the institutions** we have launched a brief survey open to the entire community since the end of 2021.

**The consultation is aimed at repository managers** who can provide technical information about software versions, technical resources and difficulties when updating their platforms.

**We are confident that this information will allow us to collaborate to bring our network of repositories to better levels of interoperability and services.**

We thank the entire community for collaborating in the dissemination of this initiative and the technical managers who answer the survey only once per institution.
