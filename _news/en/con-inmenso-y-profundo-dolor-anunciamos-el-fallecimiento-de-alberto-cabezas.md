Alberto was the executive secretary of LA Reference since 2013, leading the process that converted the original project into a stable regional network. No one did so much for our network to consolidate and gain the importance it has today. Open Science in Latin America owes a lot to Alberto Cabezas!

He worked tirelessly in search of opportunities so that science in the region had the visibility and respect it deserved. Beyond his professional achievements, those of us who make up LA Reference lost a very dear friend. His “Chilean” way of speaking, his good vibes and active energy inspired us throughout this time.

It will be very hard for us to continue without him, but we believe that the best way to honor his memory is to move forward seeking to be stronger every day. We only have words of gratitude and recognition for Alberto. Friend, thanks for everything. Rest in peace!
