**On Tuesday, October 3, UNESCO, LA Reference and LIBER organized a joint webinar with the aim of providing a cutting-edge analysis of the Open Science monitoring methods and tools currently developed in Europe**. The event introduced an inclusive track on open science, in line with the 2021 UNESCO Recommendation on Open Science.

The recording is now available on the channel [YouTube of LA Reference.](https://youtu.be/1h4JLbfHxEs)
