LA Referencia, together with the National Secretariat of Science, Technology and Innovation of Panama, the ABC platform (Access to Scientific Bibliography), the Research Data Alliance of the United States and the National University of Costa Rica, **will offer two training programs on the topic of Research Data in the month of February.**

The programs will be offered by **Heather Andrews, Data Stewardship at the Technical University of Delft, The Netherlands**. These are **asynchronous trainings through explanatory videos plus a live question and answer session with the trainer.**

#### **How ​​to participate?**

The person can take only one or both training programs, for this they must watch the videos listed below and participate in the live question and answer session with the trainer on the day detailed. The session will be broadcast openly through [LA Referencia's Facebook Live](https://www.facebook.com/LAReferenciaAA).**

* Training Program on Research Data Management in Scientific Practice: Tuesday, February 15 (8 am Central America, 9 am Panama, 11 am Brazil)
* Research Data Planning Training Program: Tuesday, February 22 (8 am Central America, 9 am Panama, 11 am Brazil)
