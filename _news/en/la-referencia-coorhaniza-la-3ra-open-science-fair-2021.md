**LA Reference is part of the organizers of the 3rd. [Open Science Fair](https://www.opensciencefair.eu/), a key OpenAIRE** initiative in the dissemination and construction of Open Science.

Open Science Fair **will take place from September 20 to 24, 2021 virtually.** This year's theme is **“Fostering local and global open science communities.”** The goal is to bring together and empower open science communities and services; identify common practices related to open science; to see what are the best synergies to provide and operate services that work for many; and bring experiences from all over the world and learn from each other.

For LA Reference this co-organization **represents recognition of our network as part of the global Open Science ecosystem.**

The registration period will be open until September 10. The call for proposals has a deadline (summary submission) until July 26.

It is also co-organized by global communities such as: COAR, EIFL, Force11, LIBER, OPERAS, Sparc and Sparc Europe.
