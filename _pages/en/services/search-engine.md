<h1>Search Engine</h1>

<p class="page-lead">The simplest way to find scientific publications.</p>

<p>LA Referencia is a tool that centralizes and facilitates the search for scientific publications in the region. Researchers, lecturers and students can find articles as well as master’s and doctoral theses from the universities and research institutions that make up the network.</p>

<p>It is possible to search quickly and easily by country, subject area, title, author or institution. The service also offers citations according to international standards and allows results to be shared through the most widely used social networks.</p>

<section class="content-section">
  <h2>How do we do it?</h2>
  <p>We work through organizational and technical processes that ensure data quality, focusing on several stages. To do this, we rely on a metadata harvesting platform developed by the team, LR Harvester, together with other open software components, including VuFind. The solution is deployed both at the central node and at each national node.</p>
  <div class="content-grid">
    <article class="content-card">
      <h3>We agree</h3>
      <p>Countries subscribe regional agreements to define common policies and quality standards in order to build a federated network of scientific documentation repositories.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="content-card">
      <h3>We harvest</h3>
      <p>We periodically collect metadata records provided by the repositories in the network through each national node, keeping portal contents up to date.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="content-card">
      <h3>We validate</h3>
      <p>LA Referencia develops mechanisms to ensure the quality of the published information, in accordance with the agreed quality guidelines.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="content-card">
      <h3>We federate</h3>
      <p>We carry out an integration and indexing process for the contents, providing users with a unified search service under shared quality criteria.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="content-card">
      <h3>We provide</h3>
      <p>Through a public search portal, we facilitate information retrieval and access to full text, in open access, within the original institutional repository. We also maintain a statistics system on contents and their impact.</p>
    </article>
  </div>
</section>
